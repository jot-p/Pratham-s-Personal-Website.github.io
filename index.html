About Me

Hello! My name is Pratham Gill, and I am a first-year student studying software development at Bow Valley College. I have always been fascinated by technology and its ability to improve our lives, and I am excited to be pursuing a career in this field.

As a software development student, I am learning programming languages such as C++,JAVA and C# as well as web development technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am also gaining experience in software design and development methodologies, such as Agile and Waterfall.

In addition to my coursework, I am passionate about exploring new technologies and applying them to real-world problems. I enjoy working on personal projects and contributing to open-source software projects, and I am always looking for opportunities to learn and grow as a developer.

Outside of programming, I enjoy playing video games, reading science fiction novels, and spending time with friends and family. I am excited about the possibilities that a career in software development holds, and I look forward to contributing to the industry in a meaningful way.
